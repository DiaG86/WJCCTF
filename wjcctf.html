The West Jefferson County Community Task Force (WJCCTF) was established by the Jefferson County Division of Environmental Health and Protection to identify environmental problems in the West Louisville area.

The Task Force focus is on environmental justice concerns of 12 neighborhoods in West Louisville. Every third Tuesday, monthly educational community meetings are held. Residents of any community are welcome to attend.

Individuals, groups or organizations wishing to present may contact the Task Force here.

The WJCCTF meets on the third Tuesday of each month at 6:00 p.m., at the NIA Center.
